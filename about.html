About EnjoymentClan

Our Story, Vision & Mission

Our Story

EnjoymentClan was founded in 2020 by DJ KennyBlacks with a vision to create a community of entertainment professionals who share a passion for bringing joy through music, dance, and events.

What started as a small gathering of friends has grown into a vibrant collective of DJs, dancers, producers, and event specialists who collaborate to create unforgettable experiences.

Today, EnjoymentClan has become synonymous with quality entertainment, hosting events across the country and developing talent that represents our core values of creativity, excellence, and community.

Our Mission

To create memorable entertainment experiences that bring people together through the universal languages of music and dance.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier entertainment collective, nurturing talent and setting new standards in the industry.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every performance, production, and interaction.

Community

We believe in the power of community and the bonds created through shared experiences.

Innovation

We constantly push boundaries and explore new creative avenues in entertainment.

Passion

We are driven by genuine passion for our craft and the joy it brings to others.
